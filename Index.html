Controls

Movement

Move up - W
Move down - S
Move left - A
Move right - D

Action

Attack - J
Launch spell - K
Cycle potions - L
Use potions - I
Open questbook - Q
Open Shop - F
Pause - P
Inventory mode - E

Shop & Auction

The shop is were you buy potions, quests (Note that quests can be earned from killing monsters), and the summon for Nadir dach, the supreme sorcerer (Beware that only well equiped knights should face off against Nadir). The Auction is were you can sell items, not


Inventory

There is two modes for the inventory, sellmode and switchmode indicted by the background color. Press E to switch between the two modes. Sellmode is where you can sell the items

Switchmode is were you can move items around the inventory or equip items to the accesories slots

Switchmode

Selling

Selling items


Step 1

Click on the item you wish to sell.

Step 2

Item sold and gold added to your character.

Selling special items


Note! Not all items shown.

Step 1

Click on the item you wish to sell.

Step 2

Wait. The item will be put up for auction and after some time the item will be sold, then gold will be added to the character.
